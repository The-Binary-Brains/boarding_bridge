What is Boarding Bridge?
BoardingBridge is a web platform designed specifically to help university students find reliable, long-term boarding options. Developed in partnership with ABC University, it bridges the gap between students and property owners, offering an efficient and secure way to discover, compare, and secure accommodation for the academic year.
